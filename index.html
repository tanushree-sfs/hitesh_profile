During the pandemic, he discovered online gaming. At first, it was fun, a way to pass the time. But soon, it became an obsession. Before he knew it, he had lost it all.

With a determined heart, he made a promise to himself: to start anew, to rebuild what he had lost. Each day was a struggle, but he refused to give up. He faced his fears head-on, learning from his mistakes along the way.
